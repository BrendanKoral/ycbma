Dine 4 Cause

Posted on 20th November, 2014 Categories: Uncategorized

On November 23, 2014, gather your friends, family and colleagues and come out to dinner at Qulinarnia for a traditional polish cuisine with a pinch of modernity. On that day, the You Can Be My Angel foundation and Qulinarnia are kicking off their inaugural event, Dine 4 A Cause.

On every third Sunday of the month, Qulinarnia will donate 15% from each bill to the You Can Be My Angel foundation to support children battling cancer. You can help make a delicious difference in the continuing fight against pediatric cancer. Simply enjoy a meal – or several – and be willing to match the donation. We invite other restaurants to join this event.

To learn more, please call 773-225-9749

Let the season of giving begin!
